Welcome to Hawaii!!!

Hawaii is a U.S. state made up of a chain of volcanic islands in the Pacific Ocean.

Capital: Honolulu (on the island of Oʻahu)

  • Location: Central Pacific, about 3,200 km (2,000 miles) southwest of the U.S. mainland
  • Islands: 8 main islands; the most visited are Oʻahu, Maui, Hawaiʻi (Big Island), and Kauaʻi
  • Known for: Beaches, volcanoes, surfing, hula, luaus, and unique wildlife
