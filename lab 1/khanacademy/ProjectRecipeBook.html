Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Cheese 2 slices
Butter 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Butter the bread slices

Step 2: Put cheese between the bread.

Step 3: Cook in a pan until the bread is golden and the cheese melts. Serve hot.

Chicken Soup

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 2 person
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 300 g
Water 1 liter
Carrots 1
Potatoes 1
Salt to taste

Step 1: Put chicken and water in a pot and bring to a boil.

Step 2: Add chopped carrots and potatoes.

Step 3: Cook until vegetables are soft. Add salt and serve hot.

Fruit Smoothie

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Banana 1
Strawberries 5-6
Yogurt 1 cup
Honey 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Peel and cut the fruit.

Step 2:Put all ingredients into a blender.

Step 3: Blend until smooth and serve cold.
